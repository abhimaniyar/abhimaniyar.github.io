Research:

My research lies at the interface of cosmology and galaxy evolution. I use multi-wavelength observations of the cosmic microwave background (CMB), the cosmic infrared background (CIB), and large-scale structure tracers to probe both the fundamental physics of the universe and the star formation history of galaxies. I also develop novel theoretical tools and cross-correlation techniques for current and upcoming cosmological surveys.
